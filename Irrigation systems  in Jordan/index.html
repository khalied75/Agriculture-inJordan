Irrigation Systems in Jordan

Innovative water management solutions sustaining agriculture in arid regions

Surface Irrigation

Traditional method where water is distributed over the soil surface by gravity. Commonly used in the Jordan Valley for field crops like wheat and barley.

35% Usage
60% Efficiency

Sprinkler Irrigation

Water is distributed through a system of pipes and sprayed into the air to simulate rainfall. Ideal for vegetable farms in the highlands.

25% Usage
75% Efficiency

Drip Irrigation

Most efficient system where water drips slowly to plant roots. Widely adopted in Jordan for fruit trees and greenhouse cultivation.

40% Usage
90% Efficiency
