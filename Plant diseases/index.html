Protecting Jordan's Agriculture

Plant diseases threaten food security and agricultural sustainability. Early identification and proper management are crucial for protecting Jordan's vital crops from devastating outbreaks.

🍄

Fungal Diseases

Powdery mildew, rust, blight, and other fungal infections

🦠

Bacterial Infections

Blight, spots, and wilts caused by bacterial pathogens

🦠

Viral Diseases

Mosaic viruses, leaf curl, and systemic infections

🐛

Pest-Related

Damage from insects leading to secondary infections

🥬

Nutritional Disorders

Deficiencies and imbalances affecting plant health

🌡️

Environmental Stress

Drought, heat, and other abiotic factors

Common Symptoms

Early warning signs of plant diseases

Leaf Spots & Discoloration

Yellowing, browning, or unusual patterns on leaves

Wilting & Drooping

Loss of turgor pressure and plant structure

Stunted Growth

Reduced development and smaller yields

Mold & Mildew

Fungal growth on plant surfaces

Prevention Strategies

Proactive measures to protect your crops

🌱 Crop Rotation

Prevent soil-borne diseases through strategic planting

💧 Proper Irrigation

Avoid overwatering and fungal growth

🔍 Regular Monitoring

Early detection through frequent inspections

🧼 Sanitation

Clean tools and equipment to prevent spread
